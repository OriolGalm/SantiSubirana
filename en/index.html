A mathematical and scientific base art, where the search for beauty would be an essential factor. A sum of specialized knowledge, based on a new culture of the image. Order and balance, the symmetry and proportion within a rich and diverse cultural theme that has a special interest. Logically organize all the formal elements that form the surface of the vase. Form, drawing, color, the world of symbols and calligraphies, with a thought that connects with the philosophical keys of your time and the creative spirit and the will of your time. Introduce concepts and visual components where geometric figures form a main reference. A study, reflection and analysis on the essence of art. The ability to communicate something new and better, the quality of generating new ideas and concepts. Philosophy and science, nature and the city, sources of permanent inspiration. A knowledge of the raw material and optimal performance of materials. A sculpture with special effects, looking for the internal dynamics of a style. A very solid grounding with ethical, poetic and formal principles that allow you to unleash the creative potential to favor an art in fullness, a graphic testimony and an identity sign with the artist's signature and brand.
